About (current) UI/UX Decks Artworks

Designer, Painter, UC Berkeley Student

I am a junior at University of California, Berkeley majoring in Interdisciplinary Studies Field with a focus on Social Media and Isolation. My specialized fields are Sociology, Psychology, and Anthropology, all with connections to Technology and Social Media. My focus of Research and Thesis is the effects of social media use on modern human interactions, specifically the isolation created by our constant needs of instant gratification and virtual community acceptance. Making social media a safer and more beneficial platform for users is my top priority.

My passion for design began with the Fine Arts. I studied different mediums throughout high school and college, but acrylic paint has always been my favorite. It requires smart planning and fast execution, a workstyle that suits me. Learning to express ideas effectively through art is a skill that has even allowed me to have my paintings hung in the Capitol Building in Sacramento.

I utilize my background in Fine Arts to guide my designs with color, perspective, and focus. Applying these skills to digital design is an exciting blend of classical understanding with modern tools. Recently, I created three original designs for #HappyPeriodBerkeley for their stickers and pins sale events as an effort to raise menstrual health awareness; all profits went towards helping homeless shelters.

Immigrating from Vietnam at a young age, I have come to value an open mind and acceptance towards others.
